# Elite Service Hub — $50M Boutique AI-Powered Contractor Network Blueprint

## I. Vision
ESH is a performance-driven ecosystem fusing human craftsmanship with AI.
- Build the most trusted, profitable, exclusive contractor network in America
- Boutique power network: ~200 elite contractors across 50 high-performing US markets
- "Intel Inside" of home services — ESH hexagonal mark = verified quality + automation excellence
- High-margin, limited-scale SaaS-enabled service business with intentional growth cap

## II. The Problem
- $500B+ US home-improvement market, one of least digitized industries
- Lead marketplaces (Angi, Thumbtack, HomeAdvisor) sell same lead to multiple contractors
- Turns skilled tradesmen into marketing victims
- Fragmented $90B advertising subset with no dominant end-to-end automation player

## III. The ESH Solution
### Core Model
- Contractors pay NOTHING upfront
- ESH handles: advertising, website, CRM, AI-driven communication
- 12-15% performance participation on closed jobs (only when homeowner funds clear)
- ESH fronts growth engine: underwrite advertising, automation build, intake labor
- Weekly commission settlement, 5-7 day cash float from internal performance reserve

### Smart Operations Mesh (SOM)
- AI Router: reads messages, detects urgency, creates tasks
- Work-Order Kanban: visual pipeline of every job stage
- Commission Ledger: auto-calculates ESH % and generates Stripe invoices
- Marketing Console: syncs spend, CPL, and ROAS for each city
- Conversation Hub: unifies SMS, voice, email, photo uploads
- 12 core trade templates with vocabulary packs and pricing scaffolds

## IV. Precision Program
- Single 15% performance partnership — no tiers
- ESH advances ~$12,500 per partner for media, automation, creative
- Response SLA: <60s in-hours, <5min after-hours
- Shared dashboards for homeowners, contractors, and ESH concierge
- Partners remain only while meeting standards; remediation sprints if they slip

## V. Membership Application
1. Performance Application — portfolio, licenses, references, territory intent (no fee)
2. Signal Audit & Interview — reputation, digital footprint, craftsmanship, capacity
3. Approval & Performance Covenant — throughput covenant, SOM access, brand kit

## VI. Controlled Scale
- Cap: 50 markets x 4 contractors = ~200 partners max
- Average market ad spend: $1,500 initial -> stable in 12 weeks
- Timeline: 12 months to 14 markets; 24 months to 50
- Market Selection Matrix: CPC, intent, permit velocity, contractor density
- Only markets with >= 25% margin headroom make the cut
- Activation Sprint: 21-day build per market

## VIII. Revenue Streams
1. **Performance Participation (Core):** 15% on closed jobs
2. **Activation Credit (Deferred):** ~$12,500 recaptured via success share in 90 days
3. **Stability Support:** Monthly sweep from earned commissions for analytics/QA
4. **Buy-Out Option:** Contractors can buy full system for ~$35K (rebuild cost higher)
5. **Platform Licensing (Future):** Franchise groups license ESH platform

## X. Marketing Strategy
- Dual-Brand: each contractor shines independently, ESH grows quietly behind
- Scarcity: capped membership creates waitlists and inflates desirability
- Community storytelling: case studies, before-and-after content

## XI. Risk Management
- Hard cap at 50 markets / 200 contractors
- Weekly commission runs, 90-day reserve
- Stripe Connect for payments, SOC 2-lite controls
- Modular AI stack with rollback runbooks
- Buy-out monetization ensures profitable exits

## XII. Exit Paths (3-5 years)
1. Strategic acquisition (HubSpot, ServiceTitan, PE roll-up)
2. Franchise licensing (ESH becomes standard OS for contractors)
3. Lifestyle retention ($5-6M annual cash flow, $100K/month personal)
