## üí∞ PRICING TIERS (Standard Recommendation)

### Budget Tier - Basic Upkeep
- **Monthly:** $8,100
- **Per Unit:** $368/unit/month (22 units)
- **Hours/Week:** 24 hours pooled facilities labor
- **Includes:**
  - Minor repairs only
  - Reactive ticket resolution
  - No proactive system management
  - No renovation or infrastructure work
- **Risk:** Keeps basic systems functioning but does not reduce long-term risk. Additional billing likely.

### Standard Tier - Full Facilities Coverage ‚≠ê **RECOMMENDED**
- **Monthly:** $14,900
- **Per Unit:** $677/unit/month (22 units)
- **Hours/Week:** 32 hours pooled facilities labor
- **Includes:**
  - Repairs, prevention, emergencies, and improvements
  - Real problem-solving (not just patching)
  - Most emergencies absorbed within the pool
  - Work orders, scheduling, tenant/vendor coordination
  - Concrete demo & patching, wall opening & repair, moisture investigation, deferred maintenance paydown, light renovations & unit improvements
  - Priority response & scheduling
  - KMJK Web Application (full suite)
- **Risk:** Shifts responsibility from owner to facilities team. Most emergencies absorbed within the pool. Adequate for majority of property needs.

### Premium Tier - Growth & Renovation (Subscription)
- **Monthly:** $19,200
- **Per Unit:** $873/unit/month (22 units)
- **Hours/Week:** 60 hours pooled facilities labor
- **Includes:**
  - Everything in Standard tier
  - Extra capacity (28 more hours/week) for renovations
  - Phased renovation plan (consistent weekly upgrades)
  - "Slow-Flip" units without large capital calls
  - Project management for pool projects
  - Separate queues for emergency vs scheduled
  - Renovation tracking across all units (real-time)
  - Turnover documentation included
- **Strategy:** We backfill routine maintenance so our core team can focus on Renovation. Variable speed, fixed cost. If maintenance is light, renovation speeds up. If maintenance is heavy, renovation slows down. Your bill stays exactly the same.

## üîÑ THE "STABILIZE & RENOVATE" STRATEGY (Premium Tier)

### Phase 1: Stabilization (Months 1-3)
- **Goal:** Learn the building, fix critical issues, determine "Renovation Velocity"
- **Action:** Focus on maintenance, identify patterns, establish baseline

### Phase 2: Phased Upgrades (Month 4+)
- **Goal:** Lock in renovation plan (flooring, fixtures) that fits weekly hour budget
- **Strategy:** Variable speed, fixed cost
  - If maintenance is light ‚Üí renovation speeds up
  - If maintenance is heavy ‚Üí renovation slows down
  - Your bill stays exactly the same ($19,200)
- **Priority:** Premium projects always skip the queue

## üì¶ WHAT'S BILLED SEPARATELY

- **Unit turnovers / make-ready:** Standard Turn $1,200/unit | Heavy Turn (quoted)
- **Materials:** 20% markup on cost; owner approval required over $250 (unless emergency/mitigation)
- **Capital remodels / large renovations:** Quoted separately unless approved from pool
- **Work above weekly pool:** Approved and scoped in advance unless emergency/mitigation
- **Optional add-ons:** Storm prep, HVAC protection, plumbing risk control, electrical scanning, exterior wash, renovation support
