## Strategic Insights for ESH / KMJK

### Opportunity: Vertical Integration
**Current Model:**
- Buy coatings from suppliers (Florida Coating Supply, etc.)
- Pay markup on materials
- Pass costs to customers

**Alternative Model (Like Ox Floors):**
- Manufacture own epoxy coatings
- Eliminate middleman markup
- Better margins (20-30% cost savings on materials)
- Competitive pricing advantage

### Cost Comparison (Hypothetical)
**Supplier Model:**
- Epoxy kit: $135-155 (retail from supplier)
- Contractor markup: 50-100%
- Customer pays: $200-300+ per kit

**Manufacturing Model (Like Ox):**
- Raw material cost: ~$40-60 per kit
- Manufacturing cost: ~$20 per kit
- Total cost: ~$60-80 per kit
- **Savings: 50-60% vs. supplier model**

### Implementation Requirements
1. **Formulation Expertise**
   - Epoxy chemistry knowledge
   - Lab testing capabilities
   - Quality control processes

2. **Manufacturing Facility**
   - Mixing and storage equipment
   - Bulk epoxy resin purchase
   - Packaging (3-gallon kits)

3. **Regulatory Compliance**
   - VOC regulations (Florida)
   - Safety standards
   - Product liability insurance

   - "Factory-direct" positioning
